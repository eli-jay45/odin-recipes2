Jollofrice

A delicious ghanaian jollofrice

Ingredients

  • rice
  • pepper
  • spices
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil water for 5 minutes
  2. wash your rice really well and put in water after it is boiled
  3. Cut your auxiliary including ingredients such as the carrots, cucumber and put it all together in one bowl
  4. add spices to the rice and water and mix together with the other ingredients
  5. wait for 20 - 25 minutes to get it well cooked.
